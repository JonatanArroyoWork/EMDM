El Molí del Montseny rural house


Ancient stone mill from the 13th century right in the heart of "El Montseny", biosphere reserve. A nature oasis, surrounded by woods and next to the Tordera river.

   Services:

  •    Shower and WC.
  •    Fully equiped kitchen.
  •    Free parking place.
  •   TV
  • Basics: Towels and linen.
  • Chimney.
  • Iron.
  • BBQ at garden area.

Rooms:
